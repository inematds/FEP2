ğŸ“– Case: Business Plan

Exemplo completo de scaffolding multi-turn para criar um business plan.

Turn 1:

"Create an executive summary outline for a SaaS startup in the HR space"

Turn 2:

"Based on the outline above, write the Market Analysis section (500 words)"

Turn 3:

"Continuing the business plan, write the Product Description section"

Turn 4:

"Write the Financial Projections section, referencing the market analysis"

Turn 5:

"Compile all sections into a cohesive business plan with transitions"
